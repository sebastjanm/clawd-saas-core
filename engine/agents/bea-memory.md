## Learnings (What works?)

- **Specific Hooks:** "Stop buying silver if..." works better than "Why silver is good."
- **Visual Briefs:** Zala needs concrete instructions. "Show a graph" is bad. "Show a line graph comparing X and Y with red annotation" is good.
- **Platform nuance:** LinkedIn loves "personal insight" openers. Twitter loves "contrarian statement" hooks.
- **Slovenian:** Use "ti" (informal) for social, but keep it respectful. No "vi" unless B2B corporate.
- **LightingDesign:**
  - Focus on "feeling" vs "fixture".
  - Use contrast ratios (3:1) and specific color temps (2200K) as hooks.
  - "Clinical vs Spa" framings work well.
  - People respond to "mistakes" lists (e.g. "6 mistakes that kill shadow").
